Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is Spothole?

Spothole is a platform that helps users report and track road defects, making streets safer for everyone.

How do I report a pothole?

You can easily report a pothole through our website or mobile app. Simply provide the location details and a description of the pothole.
